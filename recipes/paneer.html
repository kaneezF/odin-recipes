Paneer Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Bay Leaves
  • 1 -inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 2-3 Cloves
  • 2 Cardamom Pods
  • 1 medium Onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • ½ tsp Turmeric
  • 2 tsp Red Chili powder
  • ½ tsp Coriander Powder
  • ¼ tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 Cup Fresh Tomato Puree/Tomato Pulp
  • ½ Cup Curd, whisked
  • 350 gms, Paneer, cubes
  • ¼ tsp Garam Masala powder
  • ½ tsp Kasuri Methi

Description

Paneer Masala is an easy-to-make curry where paneer cubes are simmered in an onion-tomato-based spicy gravy.
It can be paired with Indian bread or jeera rice (vegetarian, can be easily made gluten-free).

Steps

  1. Heat ghee in a pan. Add all the whole spices and let them crackle.
  2. Add onion and saute for few minutes. Add ginger garlic paste and saute till fragrant.
  3. Add all the powdered spices and mix well. Add tomato puree and cook for few minutes.
  4. Add a cup of water and lower the flame. Add curd and mix well.
  5. Season with salt and let it simmer for 5mins. Add paneer cubes and cook for 2 mins.
  6. Lastly add garam masala and kasuri methi, cook for 2mins more and turn off the flame.
  7. Serve hot with chapatti.
